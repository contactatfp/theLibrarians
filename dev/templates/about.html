About Us

Our Mission Statement

To help parents who don't have time for storytime.

What our website does

A Child's Story is designed to provide parents with unique stories when they've run out of stories to read or don't have time to read a bedtime story. Our website is extremely easy to use, all you have to do is sign in, provide us with your child's name and age, the title of the story, and a brief description of the content you would like the story to be about. We use the same API as popular AI ChatGPT to come up with unique stories and imaages every use.
